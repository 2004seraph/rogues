How to Play

Offline with two players sharing a keyboard

Player 1 Controls

  • Movement: ASD
  • Jump: Space
  • Light attack: B
  • Heavy attack: N
  • Special attack: M

Player 2 Controls

  • Movement: Arrow keys
  • Jump: Numpad 0
  • Light attack: ,
  • Heavy attack: .
  • Special attack: /

Online

Each player must register and sign in to an account.

Both then click on online play, one player hosts a room and then gives the code to the other for them to join with.

To play competitively and earn rankings, you must use random matchmaking.
